## Gathern Host Portal Features Analysis

### What do we offer you in Gathern platform?
- **Additional income with little effort:** Displaying your property to over 2 million guests and hundreds of thousands of daily searches, providing wide customer reach.
- **Property protection:** Protection program for rare cases of guest damage, theft, or unusual cleaning costs.
- **Safe and regulated process:** All guests are verified through the National Information Center, and the platform is licensed by the Ministry of Tourism and operates under Saudi Arabian regulations.

### Expected Income Calculator
- Allows hosts to estimate potential monthly income based on property type and city.

### How to host?
- Simple steps: Register, add property information and photos, and be ready to receive guests.
- **Gathern Protection Program:** Details on property protection against damage, theft, and unusual cleaning costs.

### Gathern Business App
- A comprehensive platform for managing your property for free.

**Key takeaways for the proposed platform:**
- **Guest verification:** Essential for security and trust.
- **Property protection/insurance:** Crucial for attracting investors.
- **Income estimation/tracking:** Important for investor decision-making and management.
- **Property management tools:** A dedicated app/portal for hosts to manage their properties.
- **Booking management:** Implied through the hosting process, but details on approval/rejection and communication (voice messages) are not explicitly mentioned.
- **Maintenance mode:** Not explicitly mentioned.
- **Financial reports:** Not explicitly mentioned.
- **Guest reviews/feedback:** Not explicitly mentioned for hosts to see or respond to.
- **Host feedback/complaints:** Not explicitly mentioned.
